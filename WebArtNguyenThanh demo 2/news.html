Spotlight Award — Red Dot Miami Art Fair

December 2025

Nguyen Thanh was awarded the Spotlight Award at Red Dot Miami Art Fair 2025 in recognition of his large-scale abstract paintings exploring light, atmosphere, and emotional balance. The presentation marked an important international milestone within his ongoing artistic practice.

Participation at Affordable Art Fair London

October 2023

Nguyen Thanh presented a selection of recent abstract works at Affordable Art Fair London 2023, in collaboration with Hanoi Art House. The presentation introduced his atmospheric oil paintings to a wider European audience.
