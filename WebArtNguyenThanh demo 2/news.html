Nguyen Thanh Wins Spotlight Award at Red Dot Miami 2025

December 2025

Nguyen Thanh received the Spotlight Award at Red Dot Miami 2025 for his large-scale abstract works exploring light, atmosphere, and emotional balance. The presentation drew strong attention from collectors and curators.

Nguyen Thanh's recent abstract works were exhibited at the Affordable Art Fair London 2023 in collaboration with Hanoi Art House, introducing his atmospheric oil paintings to a broader European audience.
