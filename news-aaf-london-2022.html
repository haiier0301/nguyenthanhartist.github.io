Affordable Art Fair London 2022 — Selected Works

October 2022

A selection of Nguyen Thanh's paintings was presented at AAF London 2022, marking the artist's first introduction to the UK art fair scene.

The works on view demonstrated his evolving approach to abstract expressionism, featuring carefully balanced compositions that evoke atmosphere, light transitions, and meditative urban landscapes. The fair provided valuable exposure to European collectors and galleries interested in contemporary Southeast Asian art.
