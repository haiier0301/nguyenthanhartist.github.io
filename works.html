Selected Artworks

Nguyen Thanh's recent works focus on abstract expressionist oil paintings inspired by urban landscapes, rivers, horizons, and light. The compositions explore the harmony between movement and stillness, using layered textures and luminous color fields.

Each painting is part of an ongoing exploration of tranquility within motion — a visual meditation on balance, resilience, and inner calm.
