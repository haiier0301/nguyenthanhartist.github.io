Interview and Feature on VnExpress 2005

2005

An early feature on VnExpress introduced Nguyen Thanh's emerging artistic voice and his first exhibitions in Ho Chi Minh City.

The article captured the beginning of his professional practice following his graduation from the College of Pedagogy, Fine Arts Department. It discussed his early explorations in abstract painting and his aspirations to develop a distinctive visual language rooted in atmosphere and emotional expression.
