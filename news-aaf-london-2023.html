Participation at Affordable Art Fair London 2023

October 2023

Nguyen Thanh's recent abstract works were exhibited at the Affordable Art Fair London 2023 in collaboration with Hanoi Art House, introducing his atmospheric oil paintings to a broader European audience.

The presentation featured works from his "Silent Horizon" and "Urban Light" series, showcasing his exploration of luminosity, spatial rhythm, and emotional depth. The fair provided an opportunity to connect with collectors and art enthusiasts from across the UK and Europe.

This marked Nguyen Thanh's second participation at AAF London, building on the positive reception from the 2022 edition and further establishing his presence in the international contemporary art scene.
