NGUYEN THANH

Contemporary Abstract Expressionist Painter

Nguyen Thanh is a Vietnamese contemporary artist whose work explores the balance between energy and tranquility within modern urban life. Through expressive oil paintings, he captures moments of stillness emerging from movement, chaos, and light.

His practice is rooted in more than two decades of artistic exploration, moving from lyrical landscapes to powerful abstract expressionism. Today, his works are collected internationally and have been presented at major art fairs, including Red Dot Miami, where he received the Spotlight Award 2025.

The paintings offer viewers a space of calm, reflection, and emotional resonance — a quiet refuge within the rhythm of contemporary cities.
