Nguyen Thanh Wins Spotlight Award at Red Dot Miami 2025

December 2025

Nguyen Thanh received the Spotlight Award at Red Dot Miami 2025 for his large-scale abstract works exploring light, atmosphere, and emotional balance. The presentation drew strong attention from collectors and curators throughout the fair.

His booth featured recent paintings from the "Peace in the City" series, highlighting his signature approach to layered oil surfaces and atmospheric depth. The recognition marks a significant milestone in his international exhibition trajectory, following successful presentations in London and Montreal in recent years.

The Spotlight Award acknowledges exceptional artistic merit and presentation quality among participating artists. Nguyen Thanh's work resonated with the fair's diverse audience through its contemplative visual language and refined execution.
